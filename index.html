Product

EIN-SOF Networks offers a wide range of WDM (wavelength division multiplexing) and OTN (optical transport network) products, that support any mix of data, storage, video and TDM services including 10/40/100/400Gb Ethernet and 8/16/32G FC over dark fiber, WDM and OTN. Ein-Sof solutions have embedded optional encryption that enables secured transport of up to 400G per wavelength.
